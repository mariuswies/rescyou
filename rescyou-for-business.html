We supply
the world’s first smart warning triangle

Our product can be seamlessly integrated into any
in-car system to ensure maximum safety for customers.

The less stress, the better, the safer — the motivation behind rescyou. Three out of ten experienced drivers do not know how far the warning triangle must be placed behind the car in the event of a breakdown or accident. Not to mention that conventional warning

triangles aren’t easy to set up. In a stressful situation like this, it can even be dangerous. Rescyou solves this problem. It knows where the car is located and shows the driver by discreet signals how far the warning triangle must be placed according to legal regulations.

Fully integrated

Everything you need
and a little bit more

With the solution integrated by the car manufacturer, there is no need to download an additional app for our product. Rescyou is more than just another smart feature in a smart car. We not only offer a digital interface to the manufac-turer, but also advise on the positioning within the car.

Our product can be seamlessly integrated into any in-car system to ensure maximum safety for customers.

With a power connection brought forward by the car manufacturer, rescyou has a permanent power supply so that it’s ready when you need it.

The heavy battery guarantees a solid stand and ensures that the warning triangle remains stable in high wind speeds.
