The world’s first
smart warning triangle

The smart warning triangle from rescyou is designed to support drivers in case of a breakdown or accident and to relieve them as much as possible. It is the first of its kind that provides pleasant support in an unpleasant situation.

The less stress, the better, the safer — the motivation behind rescyou. Three out of ten experienced drivers do not know how far the warning triangle must be placed behind the car in the event of a breakdown or accident. Not to mention that conventional warning

triangles aren’t easy to set up. In a stressful situation like this, it can even be dangerous. Rescyou solves this problem. It knows where the car is located and shows the driver by discreet signals how far the warning triangle must be placed according to legal regulations.

handy and small

Easily positioned in your car

Rescue can be placed anywhere in your car, as long as it is accessible. It doesn't matter which kind of vehicle you drive and how old it is, as it works completely independent of the manufacturer.

safety vest

Be visible

One of the most obvious things about a breakdown is often forgotten by drivers despite legal regulations and fines for not owning one. The safety vest. To prevent this from happening in the future, it is smartly integrated in rescyou. Guys, you gotta watch your back.

Easy to use

Pull and go

Rescue is being activated by pulling the straps, which are highly visible placed in the case. No cumbersome mounting as with conventional models – it unfolds automatically. The case functions as a base station which charges and tells the warning triangle the distance in between them.

No brainer

Don't worry about
distance anymore

Since rescyou is equipped with built-in GPS, it knows where the car is located and automatically adjusts the distance according to the legal regulations in town, on freeways or highways. Therefore the driver only has to follow the distance indicator until all LED's light up.

Visual feedback

Put it down

As soon as rescyou has reached the appropriate distance from its base station, it will kindly ask you to drop it off with the help of light signals running towards the ground.

everything connected

Check your phone

It's never nice to get stuck with your car and so that you are not alone with your problem by the time, rescyou offers you a list of people who can help you out. Once you have connected your device to the smartphone app in the first place, it will immediately notice when rescyou is activated and support you.

everything set up

You’re as safe as you can be

The first big step has been taken. From here on we ask you to take care of yourself, to wait for help behind the guard rail and we hope that you will be spared such situations in the future.

Through thoughtful UX we make sure you don't waste unnecessary thoughts in situations where you need them the most.

Designed for durability, rescyou provides a standby time of three years. In case it runs low, a push notification gently sends a reminder to charge.

The heavy battery guarantees a solid stand and ensures that the warning triangle remains stable in high wind speeds.
